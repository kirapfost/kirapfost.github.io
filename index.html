I asked her how her day was and she opened up with an honest answer right back, she told me she had had a terrible morning but she insisted on continuing to "kick" and making it to the end of her shift so she could relax. I asked her what had made her morning bad and she launched into story mode, describing her irresponsible manager not opening the store on time or telling anyone to cover her. She spoke with her words and her hands as she vented all her morning drama, and at the end of the conversation she audibly inhaled deeply and it sounded like relief.
